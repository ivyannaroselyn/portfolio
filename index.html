About Me

I started my modelling journey in 2021. At first, I joined a modelling workshop in Australia. Since then, I look for Time for Print (TFP) opportunities to get more experience, build connections, and improve my portfolio.

Throughout 2022, I started to do modelling as a hobby, doing fashion style photoshoots nearly every week! These experiences improves my make up skills, fashion creativity, knowing how to pose better and giving me more creative ideas during photoshoots.

I am based in Adelaide, South Australia and will continue to expand my modeling career in nearby areas. Get in touch today and book your session with me!
